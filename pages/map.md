This map features a number of locations from PTA’s filmography. Pins are color coded by film (see map legend). Selecting a pin will produce a pop-up with a description of that location, including the address, latitude/longitude, and, when possible, a photo of the filming location taken by one of our team members. Click on the thumbnail to go to that object’s page in our collection.

This map will be updated in the future with additional locations and functionality. If you would like to contribute any additional locations, please use our [contact form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdEWojQpxRp1vWhzNjGnI-mGC8bGztAyWR1wsA27lcoSvZe3Q/viewform).
